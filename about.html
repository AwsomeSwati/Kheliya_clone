About Kheliya Toys

Welcome to Kheliya ® – your #1 source for high-quality toys, baby products, games, children books & music, stationary utilities & more. We’re dedicated to giving you the very best with a focus on highest quality, reliability, customer service and uniqueness.

After foundation in 1985, Kheliya came a long way in the past 35+ years. Now, we’ve 22 toy shops at different locations in Maharashtra. We have a plan for expanding across India. If you’re interested in partnering with us for a franchise store then you’re most welcome to apply for it here. We deliver products across India through our multiple warehouses & retail partners.

We feel proud of being your most favorite toy store chain in Maharashtra, India after serving more than 1 million happy customers.

Online shopping trend is growing & we understand it as a need of the time. KheliyaToys.com is our new endeavor as an online toy store to stay available for you, at your leisure & convenience. You can buy toys online in India without worrying about the quality of the products or shipments. We offer 100% purchase satisfaction & full refund or replacement if you receive damaged products.

We don’t just sell toys, we’re passionate about them.

We’re certain that you’ll enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you’ve any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We’d love to hear from you.
